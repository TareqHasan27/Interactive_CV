MY HOBBIES

I love the thrill of discovering new places, cultures, and stories. Traveling gives me a sense of freedom and helps me grow with every journey. It’s not just about the destination, but the memories I collect along the way.

Photography

I see photography as a way to freeze time and tell stories without words. Every shot I take reflects the beauty I find in everyday moments. It helps me express emotions and preserve memories forever.

Gaming is more than just a hobby for me it's a way to challenge my mind, connect with friends, and live out epic adventures. Whether I’m solving puzzles or battling in real-time, I enjoy the strategy, fun, and escape it offers.

Editing Videos

I love the creative process of editing videos — turning raw clips into meaningful stories. Whether it's trimming, syncing, or adding effects, I enjoy shaping content that captures emotion, energy, and purpose. It's like painting with time and sound
